void calculateBounds ( )

Calculates the d_ptr->minimum bounds and the d_ptr->maximum bounds (i.e., the d_ptr->minimum and the d_ptr->maximum displayed on the chart). Calculates the d_ptr->order and the tick size(delta between two ticks on the Y axis) of the charts values. If the d_ptr->minimum is equal to the d_ptr->maximum, the d_ptr->minimum bound is equal to d_ptr->minimum - 1 and the d_ptr->maximum bound to d_ptr->maximum + 1
